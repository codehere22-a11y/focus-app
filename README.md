Here's your phone-ready focus app. 
how to save it to your home screen:
On iPhone: Open the file in Safari → tap the Share button → "Add to Home Screen" → done. It'll appear as an app icon.
On Android: Open in Chrome → tap the 3-dot menu → "Add to Home Screen" → done.
What's inside the app:
Six tabs you'll actually use daily — a detox checklist to start, a visual schedule with a timeline, the 5 iron rules, a built-in focus timer (with 25/45/60/90 min presets), a weekly streak tracker that saves your progress, and the evening shutdown ritual.
The timer also vibrates when your session completes and counts your total sessions over time — so you can see yourself building the habit day by day.
